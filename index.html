Good To Go

At Good to Go, we're on a mission to make a positive impact on both the environment and the lives of those in need right here in the beautiful state of Kerala, India. Our initiative is a testament to the spirit of community and care that defines our culture.

Our Purpose:

We understand that excess food from parties, weddings, and functions often goes to waste. But what if we could turn this excess into a source of nourishment for the underprivileged? Good to Go bridges the gap by collecting surplus food and redistributing it to homeless and needy individuals across Kerala.

Our Commitment:

Our dedication lies in reducing food waste while addressing the pressing issue of hunger in our communities. By working together, we can ensure that no edible food goes to waste while creating a positive change in the lives of many.

Why Choose Us?

Community-Driven: We believe in the power of communities coming together to create change. With the support of event hosts, volunteers, and donors, we are turning surplus food into an instrument of compassion.

Sustainability: Our initiative is not just about immediate relief; it's about building a sustainable model that addresses long-term food wastage and hunger challenges.

Transparency: We are committed to transparency in our operations. From food collection to distribution, every step is carried out with integrity and accountability.

How it Works

Event Registration:

Event hosts and organizers register their events with us, sharing details about the surplus food.

Seamless Collection:

Our efficient team coordinates with event hosts to collect excess food after the event.

Distribution:

The collected food is carefully packaged and distributed to shelters, orphanages, and communities in need.

Nourishing Kerala, Sustainably

With each event you host and every meal you donate, you become a part of a larger movement to reduce food waste and address hunger in Kerala. Our initiative aligns with Kerala's rich tradition of sharing and caring for one another.

About Us

At Good to Go, we are fueled by a simple yet powerful belief: that no one should go to bed hungry while edible food goes to waste. With this mission at the heart of our initiative, we are dedicated to making a positive impact in Kerala, India.

Our Story:

The story of Good to Go began with a shared passion for creating a sustainable solution to two critical issues: food waste and hunger. Inspired by the rich tradition of hospitality and compassion that Kerala is known for, we embarked on a journey to bridge the gap between surplus food and those in need. Our goal is to create a future where no excess meal is thrown away and every individual has access to nourishing food.

Join Our Cause

Whether you're an event host willing to share your excess food, a volunteer looking to make a hands-on difference, or a supporter who believes in our mission, there's a place for you at Good to Go. Together, we can transform surplus into sustenance and build a stronger, more caring community.
